ABOUT ME

I have graduated from School of Graphic Design – Globlife where I got a degree of licensed computer graphic designer. Moreover I am an absolvent of Ignatianum Academy – Culture studies with Media specialization. I live and work in Cracow.

I specialize in graphic design, illustrations and video games animation which are not only the way to make a living but also – what is the most essential – my biggest passion. In addition I am creating visual identifications, mobile applications and all sort of print materials.

In my work I attach great attention to details and I am taking full responsibility for individual character and precise implementation of every project. I am offering professional consulting and realization starting from the idea and finishing on the final design.

If you have any questions fell free to contact me.
